## How to use
1. Import to your CRM/sheet. Add a "Notes" and "Owner" column.
2. Work **Tier A** first, and **always lead with warm intros** (see `03_.../warm_intro_request.md`).
3. GCC sovereigns and family offices: do **not** cold-email without an introduction where avoidable.
4. Update "Last Touch" and "Next Action" after every interaction.
5. Strategics (Autodesk etc.): approach as partnership first, investment second.

*Reminder: remove or re-tier any name once verified as out of stage/thesis. The list is a
starting hypothesis, not a fact base.*
