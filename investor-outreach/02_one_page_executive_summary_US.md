# BaytyAI — Executive Summary (US)

**Infrastructure Intelligence for the GCC. The verified operating layer for construction and
infrastructure, from approval to asset operations.**

### Problem
GCC governments and developers run the world's largest construction programmes on email,
WhatsApp, and disconnected tools. Approvals stall in inboxes. Contractor credentials go
unverified. No one can answer "who approved what, when" without an investigation. The result
is delay, rework, and dispute — measured in weeks and millions per project.

### Solution
BaytyAI is an AI-native platform that (1) verifies every stakeholder before they touch a live
project, (2) runs structured, named, timestamped approval chains with a full audit trail, and
(3) keeps one source of truth for documents and decisions. AI summarises correspondence,
extracts data from drawings and contracts, and flags risk before it becomes delay.
